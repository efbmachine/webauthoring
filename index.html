What is for

Nowadays it's becoming more and more important to protect your data. For multiple reasons:

  • You have something precious in your machine that you don't want people to access
  • To protect yourself from potential hackers
  • Simply because you don't want the government or your Internet Access Provider to see which websites you visited.
For all these reasons, Private Life exists.

To be almost completely anonymous online: you should start by setting up a VPN,
then continue by creating a Virtual Machine. Afterwards you should download Tor
on your Virtual Machine. Then you are setup to be almost undetectable on the internet. If you would like
to buy stuff from .onion sites you can then buy some bitcoins.
You could as well just go through all of then in any order you wish.
